* What functions are available when?

** ~stdenv~

Standard GNU utilities. Made available in builders by sourcing ~$out/stdenv~

** ~builtins~

Always available. In addition, ~derivation~, ~import~, ~abort~, and ~throw~ don't
need the ~builtins~ prefix.
